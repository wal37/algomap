Platform overview

The control layer for GIS, routing, and geography-linked operations.

Algomap helps logistics teams, field services, utilities, and infrastructure operators move from disconnected maps and dispatch tools into one spatial operating system. It combines where things are, where teams need to go, what happened on the ground, and what should happen next.

Map

Organize base maps, service zones, infrastructure layers, assets, and field context.

Move

Plan routes, coordinate fleets and teams, and respond faster to changes on the ground.

Review

Keep route history, spatial records, and operational outcomes tied to real geography.
